Getting advices for your self

Home



Those are some advices that can help you just press on the advice and you will see the answer.

If you have a diabetes.

Try to avoid eating any food is Sweet or contain sugars.

Such as chocolate, candy, and any food is Sweet.

If you have a Blood Pressure.

Try to avoid eating salty foods or any thing acid.

Such as salt or Lemon.

If you have both diabetes and Blood Pressure.

Try to balance between eating foods that contain sugars or Chocolate and eating salty foods and acid things.

If you feel the dizziness.

Put your hand on somthing is fixed, because the nerves of the hand gives the brain signales to reduction the dizziness.

If you suffer from dryness of mouth.

Drink water, and it will be better to drink much mineral water.

If you want to make your mind strong.

Drink cup of coffee every morning.

If you have colisetrol.

Drink juices and it will be better to drink apple juice, becuse apple juice can reduces the colisetrol better than the athor juices.

Do not over eat saturated fats can increase blood cholesterol levels.

If you want to avoid injury by cancer.

Daily walk for half an hour or an hour reduces the risk of cancer by 18% and helps to get rid of 3 kg almost a year and maintains the strength of the body.

If you want to avoid injury by heart attack.

Be sure to eat fruits and vegetables at a rate of 3 servings daily, it could reduce the risk of heart attack by 70%.
